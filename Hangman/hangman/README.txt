Hangman  Version 3.12
By Yitzchak Weiser
A simple one player game that picks a random word from a list of 56 words.
It draws a stick figure hangman.  You have 6 guesses.  Enjoy. 
Besure to mount the breezyswing.jar to your libraries or filesystems.
